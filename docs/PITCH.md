# SCOUT — 3-Minute Pitch

## The Hook (0:00–0:20)

"Ukraine is losing a $4 million tank to a $500 FPV drone every day. Current counter-UAS is $500K radar — it doesn't work indoors, doesn't work in cities, doesn't work against fiber-optic drones, and you can afford one per base. We built something different."

## The Solution (0:20–0:40)

"SCOUT is a €200 autonomous ground sensor node. It walks your perimeter, explores unknown space with AI vision, and surveils it — keeping eyes on target until interceptors arrive. No radar. No jamming. No GPS dependency. And it's not alone: SCOUT C2 is the command layer that fuses every agent into one live tactical picture — 2D map, 3D village view, and mobile operator app."

## The Demo — Phase 1: Patrol / Explore (0:40–1:10)

"SCOUT begins autonomous exploration." [Robot walks tape path]

Screen: "Exploring sector... 30% observed... 60% observed..."

Cut to SCOUT C2 admin dashboard: five agents move on the 2D tactical map. Cut to the 3D village view: same agents sweep between mesh buildings, their FOV cones painting the ground green as coverage grows.

## The Demo — Phase 2: Detect & Track (1:10–1:50)

You wave a drone cutout over the table.

Robot spots it, STOPS, turns to track.

Screen flashes: "🚨 UAV DETECTED. Type: Quadcopter. Confidence: 94%. Tracking..."

Move left → robot turns left. Move right → robot turns right.

## The Demo — Phase 3: Recon / Change Detection (1:50–2:20)

"Two hours later, SCOUT runs a second recon pass." The 3D view shows agents sweeping the village again. A yellow alert flashes: "CHANGE — Building 3, door CLOSED → OPEN." This is the SE3 Labs change-detection track: comparing before-and-after imagery to spot what moved.

## The Demo — Phase 4: Report & Scale (2:20–2:50)

"SCOUT maintains visual contact and reports real-time coordinates to command." Screen shows threat map and trajectory.

"Current counter-UAS: $500K radar, one per base, doesn't work indoors. SCOUT: €200, works anywhere, deploy 100 per base for the price of one radar. On-device AI means no cloud, no datalink, no jamming vulnerability."

## The Close (2:50–3:00)

"Built in 42 hours at EDTH Munich. We're solving two challenges: ATS GmbH 01-ats — 3D graph exploration and surveillance — and SE3 Labs Track 2 — tactical change detection. One submission-ready algorithm, one walking robot demonstration, one C2 dashboard. The same platform that patrols a base today can patrol your warehouse tomorrow. Defense-grade robotics. Consumer-grade cost."
